The drill

How to practise at home: 1. Take a neutral set up position with a ball slightly in front of your sternum 2. Now take a club you are comfortable chipping with and instead of using a conventional grip, place your lead hand below your trail hand 3. Take a few small practice swings to become comfortable with the feeling of holding the club this way 4. Once you feel confident, hit a ball 5-10 yards and try to feel that your shaft is leaning forward whilst also hitting the turf after contact with the ball 5. Now hit 10-20 small chip shots still maintaining the same grip (lead hand low) really trying to feel that shaft lean and making solid impact
