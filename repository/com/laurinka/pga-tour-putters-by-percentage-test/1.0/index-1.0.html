Preparations

Putt a tee 10 feet off the tee, than each foot from 5 to 10 feet all in one line. I.e. you have a tee at 5, 6, 7, 8, 9 an 10 feet distance.

The test

Hit 10 balls from eatch distance. PGA Tour percentages:

  • 5 feet: 92%
  • 6 feet: 83%
  • 7 feet: 79%
  • 8 feet: 72%
  • 9 feet: 71%
  • 10 feet: 61%
