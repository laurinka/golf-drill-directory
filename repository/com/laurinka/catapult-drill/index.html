The drill

    Take your normal start position and produce a regular swing but stop just before you hit the ball
    Hold that position with your chest centred, head centred and pelvis centred
    Your task is to now move the ball forward without a backswing
    If you have a good body position, the ball will travel between 20-40 yards
    The further you can “throw” the ball, the better the relationship between your hands, arms and body
    This is a difficult drill to master at first so don’t be discouraged if you find it difficult
    Try catapulting between 15-20 golf balls or until you are consistently pushing the ball over 20 yards
