Preparations

Take two clubs and put the parallel each other on the ground. They should be wide enough so that you can make a stroke with your putter inside them.

The drill

Place 10 balls at one end, swing putter straight back and you should not hit clubs with your ball, balls should leave the other end (or enter a hole at the other end).
